The Tesla car company purchased cryptocurrency and expects to start accepting it as payment in the future.

By
Keir Mudie

In a stock market filing, Tesla commented that it "updated its investment policy" in January and wants to invest in "reserve assets" such as digital currencies, gold bars, or exchange-traded funds. Tesla said it is trying to maximize the return on cash that is not used in the company's day-to-day operations.

In this regard, Tesla acquired $ 1.5 billion worth of cryptocurrency, due to which the price of Bitcoin jumped 17% to $ 44,220, which is a record level.

The company also plans to start accepting Bitcoin as a form of payment for their products soon, in accordance with current legislation and initially on a limited basis.

Musk's purchase of the cryptocurrency blew up the internet and a week ago he tweeted that Bitcoin is "on the verge" of gaining wider acceptance among investors. This is not surprising.

New rules of the game

Some analysts believe Tesla's investment could be a game changer for cryptocurrency.

“I think Tesla has taken the first step now and we will see an increase in the number of companies willing to invest in Bitcoin in the future,” said Eric Turner, vice president of market analysis for cryptocurrency research firm Messari.

"One of the largest companies in the world now owns Bitcoin, and therefore every investor who owns Tesla or even just the S&P 500 has access to it as well."

“Tesla is taking a lot of risk right now - that may not bother many investors, but certainly not the conservatives,” he said.

What is Bitcoin's fate?

It is not hard to see that Bitcoin has hit an all-time high, after numerous ups and downs, over the past decade. It is important that this year the cryptocurrency received support from large financial institutions.

The world's largest fund manager Blackrock recently changed several investment mandates to allow its funds to invest, but central banks remain skeptical about the digital currency.

Governor Andrew Bailey, manger of Bank of England warns about using Bitcoin as a payment method. “I have to be honest, it's hard to see that Bitcoin has any intrinsic value,” he said. "It can have external value because people want it." Bailey also added that he was "very nervous" about people using Bitcoin for payments, investors should understand that its price is extremely volatile.

But Elon himself explains that "It will be useful for so many people, so it is important that everyone knows about it." “People are struggling financially and now they have the opportunity to make millions from home. It's incredible! "
